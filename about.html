About Us

Crafting excellence in furniture design since 2015

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Setora Design has been at the forefront of modern furniture design. Our philosophy combines Scandinavian minimalism with contemporary aesthetics, creating pieces that are both beautiful and functional.

We believe in sustainable design practices and work closely with local artisans to create furniture that lasts generations. Each piece tells a story of craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every workspace is thoughtfully designed to inspire creativity, enhance productivity, and promote well-being. Our mission is to transform ordinary offices into extraordinary environments.

Innovation

We constantly push the boundaries of design and functionality to create furniture that meets the evolving needs of modern workspaces.

Sustainability

We are committed to environmentally responsible practices, using sustainable materials and reducing our carbon footprint.

Collaboration

We work closely with our clients to understand their unique needs and create customized solutions that exceed expectations.
